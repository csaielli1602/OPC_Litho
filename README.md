# OPC & Resolution Enhancement Explorer

This tool visualizes a simplified version of optical proximity correction (OPC), the computational lithography technique used in semiconductor manufacturing to counteract light diffraction and interference. I built this after touring semiconductor fabs and research facilities and wanting a visual, hands-on way to see why mask shapes get deliberately distorted to correct for the blur of the optics. It's meant for anyone who wants an intuitive, visual feel for this part of chip manufacturing rather than a production-accurate model. Companion piece to `Silicon_Pull/`; source comments explain what's physically exact versus stylized.

Link: https://csaielli1602.github.io/OPC_Litho/
